# Flow State

> An absorbed state of consciousness in which skill and demand are precisely matched, self-consciousness dissolves, and performance peaks — the shaman's first psycho-technology and the cognitive basis of insight.

## Vervaeke's framing

Csikszentmihalyi identified flow as the "optimal experience": demands slightly exceed skills, the feedback is tight, and failure matters. Vervaeke accepts this account of the environmental conditions but adds a structural-functional explanation: flow is an *insight cascade* — a sustained series of rapid reframings, each unlocking the next. This is why flow training enhances not just performance but the cognitive machinery for insight and implicit learning. The shaman's practices (sleep deprivation, fasting, psychedelics, extended dancing) were technologies specifically for inducing flow in real ecological and social environments, thereby ratcheting up the group's overall cognitive capacity. What makes video games addictive is that they are industrially optimized flow-induction machines — and what makes them cognitively impoverishing compared to shamanic flow is that the environment is simulated, not real (Ep 02; cognitive mechanisms developed with Adrian Bennett and Leo Ferraro).
