# Sophrosyne

> Ancient Greek virtue: the perfection of salience landscape such that vice and self-deception lose their pull — not willpower overcoming temptation (enkrateia) but second-nature disposition in which the temptation does not arise; an optimization of perspectival knowing.

## Vervaeke's framing
Vervaeke introduces sophrosyne as one of the four cardinal Greek virtues (alongside wisdom, justice, and courage), often poorly translated as "temperance" or "moderation" — neither captures it. The key contrast is with enkrateia (self-control): (Ep 45)

**Enkrateia (Tom)**: Sees the temptation to lie; sees the benefits of lying; and exercises willpower to resist it. To be commended — but the virtue is still in a process of struggle.

**Sophrosyne (Susan)**: Sees the opportunity and the advantage — but lying is "unthinkable" in Frankfurt's sense: not literally inconceivable, but not a live existential option. The temptation does not arise as a temptation. Susan is more honest than Tom, because honesty has become her second nature.

This is the child-to-adult-to-sage continuum again: the adult is no longer tempted by children's toys — not because they suppress the desire but because the desire has simply evaporated. The sage's salience landscape naturally organizes toward seeing through illusion and into reality, away from the prevalent forms of self-deception, just as the adult's has naturally organized away from children's toys. "You're tempted, you're naturally tempted to the good." (Ep 45)

Augustine's formulation: "Love God and do what you want." If agape is genuinely flowing through you, sophrosyne follows — the salience landscape is so attuned to what is genuinely good that acting rightly requires no self-suppression. Sophrosyne is agape made into a dispositional form.

Sophrosyne is directed toward the three M's in Vervaeke & Ferraro's model: **morality** (not rule-following but the capacity for being virtuously responsive), **meaning in life** (the lived connection to what is genuinely meaningful), and **mastery** (excellent coping and caring through cultivated psychotechnologies and roles). And it is, at the highest level, an **optimization of perspectival knowing** — one's agent-arena relationship and salience landscape have been fundamentally reoriented through wisdom cultivation (Ep 45).
