# Wise Cultivation of Enlightenment

> Vervaeke's synthesizing practical schema: wisdom and enlightenment integrated, directed toward enhanced religio and sacredness, situated within a scientific worldview and a co-operative network of communities of practice.

## Vervaeke's framing
WCE is the positive answer Vervaeke proposes for how we can individually and collectively awaken from the meaning crisis. It names the nexus where the series' central concepts converge: wisdom produces the counteractive dynamical system that overcomes self-deception and parasitic processing; enlightenment names the cultivated shift away from modal confusion; and their overlap generates enhanced religio — the re-binding of self to the ground of being — which in turn connects to sacredness and thereby to a richly structured meaning in life (Ep 46).

WCE is not purely individual. It must be **situated within two contexts**:

1. **Worldview attunement** — the practice is embedded in a scientific, naturalistic worldview (grounded in RR machinery and 4E cognitive science) that is not hostile to religion but is not dependent on it, and equally is not dependent on any political ideology. The cognitive science provides the "why this works" account that keeps WCE from becoming mere tradition-following.

2. **Co-op network of communities of practice** in reflective equilibrium with a **wisdom wiki** — a bottom-up/top-down coordination structure: practitioners contribute lived wisdom upward; researchers contribute systematic theory downward; the wisdom wiki distills and integrates both (taking on a credo function but always in service of religio). This social dimension is not aspirational — Vervaeke argues it is already nascent in existing communities (Ep 46).

The structure of WCE tracks the structure of the account throughout the series: wisdom addresses the cognitive-existential transformations; enlightenment names the overcoming of the perennial problems via a counteractive dynamical system; religio/sacredness names the mode of relating to ultimate reality that is lost in the meaning crisis and recovered through WCE. The three reinforce each other dynamically.
