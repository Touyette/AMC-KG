# Attunement

> Heidegger's Stimmung: a pre-cognitive, non-subjective fittedness-to-being that grounds the agent-arena relationship and makes meaningful experience possible; Vervaeke maps this onto relevance realization.

## Vervaeke's framing
Heidegger introduces attunement as the level of our engagement with being that underlies both propositional truth (correctness) and even the agent-arena relationship that makes correctness possible. Vervaeke's schema: propositional correspondence is grounded in the agent-arena relationship, which is grounded in attunement (Ep 47).

Heidegger is explicit that attunement is NOT a subjective feeling or experience: "being attuned, attunement can never be understood as experience and feeling... it is thereby simply deprived of its essence." If you interpret attunement as an inner mood or emotion, you have missed it. Attunement is what makes feeling and experience *possible* — it is the prior fittedness-to-being in which any particular feeling is always already embedded. This is why it cannot be captured propositionally (Ep 47).

Vervaeke maps attunement directly onto **relevance realization**: both are pre-propositional processes that make meaningful experience possible by shaping what counts as relevant in a given environment. Just as RR is not an experience but the machinery that determines what shows up as salient, attunement is not a feeling but the existential-ontological condition that shapes how being discloses itself to Dasein. This is why Dreyfus, interpreting Heidegger, identifies "assigning relevance" as the key non-propositional, non-computational achievement that defeats the frame problem (Ep 47).

**Ek-sistence and salience**: Heidegger plays on the word *ek-sistence* (standing out, eksistent exposedness into being). What "stands out" for Dasein is shaped by attunement — the existential analogue of the salience landscape. Things don't stand out neutrally; they stand out as mattering, as calling for response, as threatening or inviting — shaped by the attuning relationship with being (Ep 47).

**Attunement → aletheia**: The path from attunement to truth as aletheia: when one does not forget attunement (the being mode is maintained), aletheia becomes possible — the disclosure of things as simultaneously shining into experience AND withdrawing beyond any frame. Forgetting attunement (forgetfulness of Being) traps one at the propositional level — truth as mere correctness — and cuts off contact with the moreness of things (Ep 47).
