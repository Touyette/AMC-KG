# Enlightenment (Reverse-Engineered)

> Vervaeke's operational definition: enlightenment is the developmental process that gives reliable amelioration and alleviation of the Perennial Problems; not an unachievable superlative for ancient supermen but a human developmental trajectory that can be understood and cultivated using the machinery of Relevance Realization.

## Vervaeke's framing
Vervaeke introduces this definition (Ep 36) as a deliberate methodological move: instead of treating enlightenment as a vague, impossible ideal belonging only to the distant past, define it operationally in terms of what it does. Enlightenment = reliable responsiveness to the perennial problems.

The project of **reverse-engineering enlightenment** uses the theoretical machinery of RR/Religio/Sacredness to ask: what were the wisdom traditions actually doing? What cognitive-existential transformations did their practices bring about? Why did those transformations help with the perennial problems? And how can we reconstruct those practices in a form that (a) is compatible with the scientific worldview, (b) does not require supernatural metaphysics, and (c) draws on the best of cognitive science, dynamical systems theory, and 4E cognitive science?

The definition is deliberately broad: "reliable amelioration and alleviation" of the perennial problems. This encompasses:
- Not necessarily complete or permanent resolution (the perennial problems are, by definition, ineliminable — they are features of the machinery, not bugs to be patched)
- A *developmental* process — not a state achieved once, but ongoing growth in the capacity to navigate the structural vulnerabilities
- "Reliable" — not accidental insights, but durable transformations through practices and ecologies of psycho-technology

The historical traditions (Buddhism, Stoicism, Neoplatonism, Christianity, etc.) are data points: each represents a culturally specific attempt to develop practices for reliable alleviation of some cluster of perennial problems, embedded in a mythos that made those practices intelligible and motivating. Reverse-engineering means identifying the underlying cognitive-existential mechanisms rather than just the cultural packaging (Ep 36).

Vervaeke's target: provide an account of enlightenment that integrates (1) the response to the historical factors that fragmented the three orders and thwarted worldview attunement, and (2) the response to the perennial problems — both addressed through the same RR-grounded account of Religio, Sacredness, and transformative practice.
