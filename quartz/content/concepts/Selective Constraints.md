# Selective Constraints

> Conditions that reduce the options available to a self-organizing system — the other half of the virtual engine structure alongside enabling constraints.

## Vervaeke's framing
Juarrero identifies selective constraints as the conditions that reduce the space of possibilities in a self-organizing feedback cycle — like resource scarcity in Darwinian evolution, which means not everything can live and so there is differential reproduction. Without selective constraints, a self-organizing system would expand into every possibility indiscriminately and would not develop in a directed fashion. The interplay of selective constraints (ruling out) and enabling constraints (opening up) creates the accordion-like motion of evolution and development. Applied to character cultivation, virtues also function as selective constraints: the virtue of courage rules out both cowardice and recklessness, narrowing the space of responses to those that are appropriately bold. The Golden Mean is a practical method for calibrating both kinds of constraint in one's character (Ep 06).
