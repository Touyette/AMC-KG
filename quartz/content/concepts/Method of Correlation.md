# Method of Correlation

> Tillich's theological methodology: an ongoing anagogic resonance between existential questioning/questing and the revelation of the depths of reality — not the matching of propositions but a mutual fitting-together that requires profound transformation.

## Vervaeke's framing
Tillich's method of correlation is widely misread as propositional theology: finding which Bible texts answer which existential questions. Vervaeke argues this trivializes Tillich. The method is not about propositions at all — if you are not undergoing a profound transformation, you are not doing Tillich's correlational method (Ep 50).

The method has two poles in tonos (creative polar tension):
- **Existential questioning** (= existential *questing*): the depths of reason — all the relevance realization machinery, aspirational rationality, recursive rationality — experienced as *ekstasis*: standing beyond oneself, at the depths of the psyche not merely psychologically but at the grounding depths of rationality itself.
- **Revelation**: the way the depths of reality reveal themselves — the *shining* (what Heidegger calls miracle: the thing shining into intelligibility) and the *withdrawal into moreness* (mystery: the inexhaustible depth that cannot be fully framed).

These two poles resonate anagogically: the depths of reason (ekstasis) cycle with the depths of reality (shining + withdrawal) in a mutual, ongoing fitting-together that can never be exhausted. This is not a one-time alignment but a continuing anagoge — the method of correlation IS the anagogic loop (Ep 50).

**Symbol's role**: The mediating element between ekstasis and miracle/mystery is the *symbol* in Corbin's imaginal sense. Symbols open up levels of reality otherwise closed; open up levels of the self otherwise closed; and do this in a mutually affording, resonant fashion. They are not made by us but self-organize out of the unconscious within us and without. Symbols can die (fundamentalism and literalism are failed attempts to prevent symbol death). They have a surplus of meaning, a numinous character, and are deeply transformative — because the method of correlation in which they participate is a transformation, not a transaction (Ep 50).

**Existential vs essential self**: The method is realized — both internalized and actualized — through the relationship between the existential self (S1: current, particular, finitely situated) and the essential self (S2: the self in the fullness of being, normatively ahead, beckoning the existential self forward). This is the same structure as the sacred second self / divine double, now rendered in Tillich's existentialist-theological vocabulary. Tillich explicitly notes the essential self is ahead causally (normatively, not causally ahead), constantly tempting the existential self toward fuller being (Ep 50).
