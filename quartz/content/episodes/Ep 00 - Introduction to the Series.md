# Ep 00 - Introduction to the Series

## What this lecture argues

Vervaeke opens by naming the problem: a civilizational meaning crisis expressed as rising anxiety, depression, disconnection from self, others, world, and future. He argues the crisis is not merely social or political but deeply historical, cognitive, and cultural — and that confronting it requires penetrating carefully into the past to recover resources. The series will draw on ancient philosophers, religious figures, cognitive scientists, and neuroscientists, tracing a connected argument across history.
