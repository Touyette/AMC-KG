# Ep 30 - Relevance Realization Meets Dynamical Systems Theory

## What this lecture argues
Ep 30 explains why relevance has no scientific theory (it lacks homogeneity, stability, and intrinsicality — like "white things" or "Tuesday events") but Relevance Realization does. The Darwin analogy is pivotal: just as biological fitness has no essence yet evolution is a rigorous theory of how fitedness constantly evolves through a virtual engine of selection and variation, Relevance Realization is Cognitive Interactional Fittedness — constantly evolving through a virtual engine operating on the bio-economy. The brain is a bio-economy managing resources (time, metabolic energy, attention) under logistical norms of Efficiency and Resiliency. These two are in an opponent-processing relationship: efficiency acts as a selective constraint (cutting the search space), resiliency acts as an enabling constraint (maintaining flexibility). Their opponent-processing virtual engine constantly optimises the sensory-motor loop's fittedness to the environment — producing what we experience as relevance without any intelligence presupposed in the mechanism.

## Concepts introduced or developed
- [[Cognitive Interactional Fittedness]] — introduced: Vervaeke's term for relevance understood as dynamic, evolving fit between cognitive processes and environment; analogous to biological fitness
- [[Bio-Economy]] — introduced: the organism's self-organizing management of limited resources (attention, metabolic energy, processing power) under logistical norms
- [[Efficiency vs Resiliency]] — introduced: the fundamental trade-off governing the bio-economy; efficiency is a selective constraint, resiliency an enabling constraint; their tension drives RR evolution
- [[Opponent Processing]] — extended (first introduced Ep 09): the autonomic nervous system exemplifies this; sympathetic and parasympathetic work in opposition yet are functionally interdependent, producing optimal arousal
- [[Relevance Realization]] — deepened: distinguished from relevance detection and projection; grounded in dynamical systems theory as an ongoing self-organizing process
- [[Virtual Engine]] — extended: the efficiency–resiliency opponent-processing system is a virtual engine that regulates the sensory-motor loop
