# Alicia Juarrero

> Contemporary philosopher who developed the concept of context-sensitive constraints and the virtual engine as a way to resolve Kant's problem about circular explanation in biology using Aristotelian ideas.

## Role in the argument
Juarrero provides the bridge between Aristotle's act/potency distinction and contemporary Dynamical Systems Theory. She distinguishes causes from constraints: causes are events that make things happen; constraints are conditions that shape what is possible. Among constraints she distinguishes enabling constraints (that open up possibilities, like genetic variation in evolution) from selective constraints (that reduce possibilities, like resource scarcity). A virtual engine arises when a systematic relationship between enabling and selective constraints regulates a self-organizing feedback cycle, producing growth and development that cannot be explained by simple linear causation. Vervaeke, extending her work with collaborators, applies this directly to the cultivation of wisdom and character: your character is your virtual engine, and cultivating virtues is cultivating the enabling and selective constraints that regulate your development as a rational being (Ep 06).
