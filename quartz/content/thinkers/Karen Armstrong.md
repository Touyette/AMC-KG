# Karen Armstrong

> Religious historian who popularized Axial Age scholarship.

## Role in the argument

Armstrong's *The Great Transformation* (2006) brought the Axial Age concept to wide readership. Vervaeke draws on her synthesis as background for his own cognitive account of why the Axial Revolution occurred and what it produced. His contribution beyond Armstrong is the structural-cognitive explanation: second-order thinking and abstract symbolic reasoning as the exapted products of alphabetic literacy and coinage, rather than a purely religious or cultural narrative (Ep 02).
