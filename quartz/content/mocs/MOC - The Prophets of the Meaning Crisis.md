# MOC - The Prophets of the Meaning Crisis

Vervaeke's five "prophets" — thinkers who independently diagnose and respond to the meaning crisis from inside modernity. Eps 46–50. Each offers something the others lack; the series closes by arguing that RR theory can broker a synoptic integration between them.

---

## The Prophet Schema

Each prophet is evaluated against a shared set of questions:
1. **Diagnosis**: What is the structure of the meaning crisis?
2. **Contact epistemology**: How do we restore genuine contact with reality?
3. **The sacred second self**: What is the aspirational self structure?
4. **Imaginal access**: How is the anagogic journey navigated?
5. **Practices**: What psychotechnologies are provided?
6. **Non-theism**: Does the framework transcend theism/atheism?

---

## [[Martin Heidegger]] (Eps 46–47)

**What he contributes**
- [[Dasein]]: being whose being is in question — the right starting point for self-inquiry
- [[Attunement]] (Stimmung): pre-cognitive grounding of agent-arena relationship ≈ RR; more fundamental than perception
- [[Truth as Aletheia]]: truth as disclosure/uncovering; the two poles (attunement-side relevance + thing-beyond-itself moreness)
- [[Forgetfulness of Being]]: the epochal root of the meaning crisis; modal confusion at civilizational scale
- [[Onto-Theology]] → [[Nihilism]]: how the philosophical tradition produced the crisis it failed to solve
- [[Thing Beyond Itself]]: objects simultaneously shine into intelligibility AND withdraw into inexhaustible depth
- [[Contact Epistemology]]: through [[Edmund Husserl|Husserl]] and [[Hubert Dreyfus|Dreyfus]]; 4E cognitive science as partial repair

**What he lacks**
- Practices: Gelassenheit (letting-be) is the posture but not a psychotechnology
- Leaves out [[Participatory Knowledge]]: his phenomenology still carries too much Cartesian grammar
- Political: his Nazi party membership is an ambivalence the series notes but does not resolve

**Key concepts**: [[Dasein]], [[Attunement]], [[Truth as Aletheia]], [[Forgetfulness of Being]], [[Onto-Theology]], [[Contact Epistemology]], [[Thing Beyond Itself]]

---

## [[Henry Corbin]] (Ep 48)

**What he contributes**
- [[Imaginary vs Imaginal]]: the single most important distinction for the prophets arc — imaginal = transjective, mediating intelligible/sensible, transformatively transframing; imaginary = merely subjective
- [[Symbol]] theory: four imaginal properties (translucent, transjective, trajective, transformative, transtemporal)
- [[Divine Double]]: the Stang motif of the self one aspires to become; Corbin's angel as its imaginal symbol
- [[Gnosis]] as dynamic coupling: self-knowing and world-knowing interpenetrate; "like known by like"
- Bridges Heidegger and Islamic Neoplatonism; the mundus imaginalis as ontologically real third realm
- Non-theism implicit: opposes fundamentalism and literalism throughout

**What he lacks**
- The body and community: the imaginal is present but embodied practices in communities are underdeveloped
- Intra-psychic tendency on the other side: too focused on individual inner transformation

**Key concepts**: [[Imaginary vs Imaginal]], [[Divine Double]], [[Gnosis]], [[Non-Logical Identity]]

---

## [[Carl Jung]] (Ep 49)

**What he contributes**
- [[Archetypes]]: virtual engines of salience landscaping (NOT mere images); imaginal, not imaginary
- [[Individuation]]: lifelong organic/autopoietic ego-Self dialogue through imaginal archetypes; the anagogic loop
- Ego vs Self: ego = archetype of conscious mind; Self = archetype of archetypes = the sacred second self
- Organic/autopoietic vs hydraulic (Freud): psyche is self-organizing, participatory, not pressure-managed
- **Practices**: active imagination, dream interpretation — the most concrete psychotechnologies of all five prophets
- Parallel with Corbin: both oppose fundamentalism/literalism for the same reason; both at Eranos

**What he lacks**
- Kantian/intra-psychic limitation: [[Martin Buber|Buber]] — can't properly account for I-Thou encounter; [[Henry Corbin|Corbin]] — imaginal should disclose the depths of the *world*, not only the psyche
- Vervaeke's synthesis: Jung provides the psychology (how internalization works) that Corbin lacks; Corbin provides the transjective/world-disclosive dimension that Jung undersells

**Key concepts**: [[Archetypes]], [[Individuation]], [[Paradox of Self-Creation]], [[Divine Double]], [[Non-Logical Identity]]

---

## [[Paul Tillich]] (Ep 50)

**What he contributes**
- [[Faith]]: ultimate concern; participatory, aspirational, not propositional; Hebrew da'at structure
- [[Ground of Being]]: non-onto-theological name for God; ground of meaning, reality, and their relationship simultaneously
- [[Method of Correlation]]: anagogic loop between existential questioning/ekstasis and revelation/depths of reality; the transformation, not proposition alignment
- [[Non-Theism]]: "God beyond the God of theism"; rejects four shared premises of theism and atheism
- [[Epektasis]] (Gregory of Nyssa): infinite self-transcendence; no resting in the ground of being; transjectivity never stops
- Existential vs essential self: = S1 vs sacred second self; essential self is normatively ahead
- Theonomy: resolves the aspiration paradox (neither heteronomous nor autonomous, but participatory in the ground of being)
- **Criticizes fundamentalism/literalism as idolatry**: deepens Heidegger's onto-theology critique theologically
- Way to live: courage as virtue + faith as ultimate concern = the most practically orientated of the prophets except Jung

**What he lacks**
- Practices: no active imagination equivalent; better than Heidegger on how to live, but no psychotechnologies

**Key concepts**: [[Faith]], [[Ground of Being]], [[Method of Correlation]], [[Non-Theism]], [[Epektasis]]

---

## [[Owen Barfield]] (Ep 50)

**What he contributes**
- [[Final Participation]]: recovery of original participation integrated with rational science; the most explicit response to the meaning crisis
- [[Infinity of Reality]]: inexhaustible moreness (from Schlegel); the combinatorially explosive surplus that draws finite beings into self-transcendence
- [[Poiesis]]: participatory creativity as ekstasis; neither passive (Gelassenheit) nor active (Cartesian will); synergistic
- Durchbruch (breakthrough) + Gelassenheit: Barfield adds what Heidegger forgot from Eckhart
- Language as trace of original participation: dual inner/outer meanings (pneuma/spiritus) as imaginal residue
- Re-exaptation as poiesis: imaginal as meeting point of sensory-motor and abstract-intellectual

**What he lacks**
- No science of meaning cultivation: calls for the integration of rational science with final participation but doesn't do it
- Evolutionary hypothesis may be wrong: Lakoff/Johnson show dual meanings are synchronic psychological development, not ancient evolutionary state

**Vervaeke's self-placement**: RR theory is the science of meaning cultivation that completes Barfield's project from the scientific side

**Key concepts**: [[Final Participation]], [[Poiesis]], [[Infinity of Reality]]

## The Synoptic Synthesis

| | Heidegger | Corbin | Jung | Tillich | Barfield |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Diagnosis | Forgetfulness of Being | Loss of imaginal | Loss of soul (sacred second self) | Idolatry / onto-theology | Loss of original participation |
| Contact epistemology | Aletheia / attunement | Imaginal / gnosis | Active imagination | Faith / method of correlation | Poiesis / final participation |
| Sacred second self | Essential self (implicit) | Divine double / angel | Self (archetype of archetypes) | Essential self (explicit) | The infinite (implicit) |
| Imaginal access | Thing beyond itself | Symbol / angel | Archetypes | Imaginal symbol / icon | Language / creative ekstasis |
| Practices | None | None (implicit) | Active imagination, dreams | None | Poiesis (no method) |
| Non-theism | Implicit | Implicit | Implicit | Explicit | Probable |

The integration Vervaeke proposes: RR theory unifies the cognitive-scientific substrate (attunement ≈ RR; salience landscaping ≈ archetypes; the thing beyond itself ≈ combinatorial explosion) while the imaginal/symbol/gnosis tradition provides the transformative depth dimension that pure cognitive science misses. The WCE framework ([[Wise Cultivation of Enlightenment]]) is the practical proposal.
