# MOC - Relevance Realization

The central cognitive mechanism in Vervaeke's framework. This MOC maps the concept, its grounding, its manifestations, and its connection to meaning and sacredness.

---

## The Core Claim

[[Relevance Realization]] (RR) is the cognitive process by which organisms home in on what matters in an open-ended environment. It is Vervaeke's answer to the [[Frame Problem]]: given [[Combinatorial Explosion|combinatorially explosive]] possibility spaces, no system can enumerate all relevant factors — it must *home in*, resonate, be drawn. RR is this homing-in capacity (Ep 10, 28).

---

## Why RR is necessary

- The [[Frame Problem]]: how a cognitive system determines what to update when the world changes (McCarthy & Hayes). Any system that enumerates will explode.
- [[Combinatorial Explosion]]: the possibility space of framings grows faster than any enumeration strategy can handle.
- [[Finitary Predicament]]: cognitive systems are finite; they cannot execute unlimited inference (Cherniak).
- [[Bounded Rationality]]: Simon's satisficing — agents can only search locally, need fast-and-frugal [[Heuristics]].

RR is the mechanism that makes heuristics possible: it is the capacity that selects *which* heuristics to use in *which* situations, and evaluates the quality of problem framings before and independent of inference.

---

## RR and the four kinds of knowing

[[The 4 Kinds of Knowing|The four kinds of knowing]] (propositional, procedural, perspectival, participatory) all depend on RR but at different levels:
- Propositional: RR selects which propositions are relevant to evaluate
- Procedural: RR calibrates which skill patterns to activate
- Perspectival: RR shapes *what it is like* to be in a situation
- Participatory: RR grounds the conformity between knower and known ([[Conformity Theory of Knowing]])

[[Normativity on Construal]]: the normative standard for problem *formulation* (how a problem is framed) is independent of the standard for problem *solution* — and this is the domain of RR. Insight occurs when RR restructures the problem frame, not when inference succeeds within a fixed frame.

---

## The RR cascade: fluency → insight → flow → higher states → enlightenment

The [[Continuity Hypothesis]] (Vervaeke): these are not different phenomena but a continuum of the same exapted cognitive machinery:

1. **[[Cognitive Fluency|Fluency]]**: ease of cognitive processing; fluency spikes feel like insight (can mislead)
2. **[[Insight]]**: sudden restructuring of problem frame; RR at the meta-level; involves [[Attentional Scaling]] (breaking inappropriate frame) + moving through to new frame
3. **[[Flow State]]**: optimal experience (Csikszentmihalyi); demands just exceed skill; absorbed engagement; RR and problem-solving are maximally coupled
4. **[[Higher States of Consciousness]]**: beyond ordinary waking; the machinery of RR exapted for non-ordinary modes of attention and self-awareness
5. **[[Enlightenment (Reverse-Engineered)]]**: the culmination; sustained transformation of the machinery of RR and participatory knowing; not a belief-change but a participatory-perspectival rebirth

Mechanism at each level: [[De-centering]] + [[Deautomatization]] → meta-perspectival restructuring → new framing emerges → absorbed re-engagement.

---

## RR and the salience landscape

[[Salience Landscape]] (Vervaeke): four layers of how consciousness foregrounds what matters:
- **Salience**: what pops out
- **Presence**: what is vividly real
- **Depth**: what has significance (below surface)
- **Significance**: ultimate mattering

The salience landscape is shaped by RR machinery operating across all four dimensions simultaneously. [[Sophrosyne]] is the virtue of a naturally well-calibrated salience landscape — superior to enkrateia because no suppression is needed.

---

## RR and the agent-arena relationship

[[Agent-Arena Relationship]]: the reciprocal structural fit between the organism's cognition and its environment. RR is the mechanism by which this fit is maintained and updated. [[Worldview Attunement]] is the large-scale version: alignment of one's worldview with one's immediate experience of meaning.

RR is transjective ([[Transjectivity]]): it is not in the agent or in the environment but in the *relationship* between them. This is why relevance is neither purely subjective ("I find it interesting") nor purely objective ("it has measurable importance") but a real organism-environment relation.

---

## RR and the prophets

The prophets arc (Eps 46–50) can be read as a series of convergences on RR:
- **Heidegger's [[Attunement]]** = pre-cognitive RR; Vervaeke explicitly maps attunement to RR (Ep 47)
- **Corbin's [[Imaginary vs Imaginal|imaginal]]** = the mode in which RR operates when it mediates between intelligible and sensible
- **Jung's [[Archetypes]]** = virtual engines of salience landscaping = structural-functional RR modulators
- **Tillich's [[Method of Correlation]]** = the anagogic cycle of RR applied to the deepest level (existential question ↔ revelation)
- **Barfield's [[Infinity of Reality]]** = the combinatorially explosive surplus that makes RR perpetually necessary; final participation = RR properly attuned to that surplus
- **[[Final Participation]]** = what it looks like when RR is exercised in the participatory mode, freed from the Cartesian grammar that traps it in the propositional/having mode

---

## RR and sacredness

[[Sacredness]] is the transjective property of inexhaustibleness — the quality of being-that-draws-one-into-self-transcendence. In Vervaeke's framework: something is sacred when the RR process encounters an inexhaustible surplus in it; the sacredness is the property of the *relationship* (transjective), not a supernatural addition to the object or a mere subjective feeling. [[Wonder]] is the affective face of this encounter; [[Numinous|the numinous]] (Otto) is its full structure (mysterium tremendum et fascinans).
